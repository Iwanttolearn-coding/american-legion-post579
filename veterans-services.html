Free Benefits Help — No Membership Required

Post 579 Service Officers provide completely free, confidential VA claims assistance to all veterans in our community. You don't have to be a member to get help.

Request Assistance
⚖️

VA Disability Claims

Free help filing, documenting, and appealing VA disability compensation claims. We help you build the strongest possible case for the benefits you've earned.

🏥

VA Healthcare Enrollment

Help enrolling in VA healthcare, finding VA facilities near you, and understanding your healthcare options and copays as a veteran.

🎓

Education Benefits

GI Bill guidance, STEM programs, vocational rehabilitation, and education benefits counseling for veterans, dependents, and survivors.

🏠

Housing Assistance

VA home loan information, housing instability referrals, HUD-VASH voucher guidance, and emergency housing resources in Bexar County.

💼

Employment Support

Job placement referrals, vocational rehabilitation (Chapter 31), Hiring Our Heroes connections, and transitioning service member resources.

🧠

Mental Health Resources

PTSD and MST resources, Vet Center referrals, peer support connections, and the Veterans Crisis Line (988, Press 1) for immediate help.

👨‍👩‍👧

Survivor Benefits

DIC (Dependency and Indemnity Compensation), survivor benefit plans, burial allowances, and support for surviving spouses and dependents.

⛓️

Incarcerated Veterans

VA benefits restoration, legal aid referrals, discharge upgrade guidance, and post-release reintegration support for veterans involved in the justice system.

🍽️

Food & Emergency Help

Food bank referrals, emergency financial assistance applications, Salvation Army coordination, and community resource navigation for veterans in crisis.
