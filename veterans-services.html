Free assistance, advocacy, and resources for every veteran at Post 579 — regardless of membership status.

How Post 579 Helps Veterans

Our service officers and volunteers are ready to assist veterans with navigating the VA system and accessing the benefits they earned.

Free Help

VA Benefits & Claims

Free assistance filing VA disability claims, appeals, and benefits applications. Our accredited service officers guide you through the entire process.

Healthcare

VA Healthcare Navigation

Help enrolling in VA healthcare, understanding your eligibility, and connecting with the right VA medical facilities and community care options in San Antonio.

Housing

Housing Assistance

Information and referrals for veterans facing housing insecurity — including HUD-VASH, transitional housing, and emergency shelter resources.

Employment

Employment Support

Resume assistance, job fairs, employer connections, and referrals to veteran employment programs in San Antonio and Bexar County.

Mental Health

Mental Health Resources

Connection to PTSD treatment, counseling, peer support programs, and the Vet Center in San Antonio. No veteran should suffer alone.

Education

GI Bill & Education

Help understanding and using GI Bill benefits, scholarship opportunities, and vocational rehabilitation programs through the VA.
