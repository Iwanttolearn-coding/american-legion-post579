Veterans Services

Post 579 is here to fight for the benefits you earned. Free. Confidential. For every veteran.

Veterans Crisis Line — If You're in Crisis, Call Now

Dial 988 and press 1 — available 24/7. Text 838255. Chat at VeteransCrisisLine.net. You are not alone. Post 579 can also connect you with local mental health resources.

How We Help Veterans

VA Benefits Guidance

Our accredited Service Officers help veterans understand and access their full range of VA benefits — healthcare, disability compensation, pension, education, home loans, and more.

Disability Claims Assistance

We help veterans file VA disability claims, gather supporting documentation, and navigate the ratings process — at no cost, ever. Our Service Officers are trained and accredited.

Appeals Support

If your VA claim was denied or your rating is incorrect, we help you understand your appeal options and build a stronger case for reconsideration.

Housing & Homelessness Resources

We connect veterans experiencing housing insecurity with local shelters, HUD-VASH vouchers, and transitional housing programs in the San Antonio area.

Food Assistance

No veteran should go hungry. We provide referrals to local food banks, emergency assistance programs, and veteran-specific food support initiatives.

Employment Services

Transitioning out of the military? We connect veterans with job placement programs, vocational rehabilitation, and employers who value military service.

Mental Health & PTSD

We refer veterans to VA mental health services, Vet Centers, community counseling, and peer support groups for PTSD, substance use, and trauma recovery.

Incarcerated Veterans

We advocate for veterans who are currently incarcerated or recently released, connecting them with legal aid, VA benefits restoration, and reintegration support.

Request Veteran Assistance

Submit this form and a Post 579 Service Officer or representative will contact you. All information is confidential.

Veteran Assistance Request
All requests are reviewed promptly. For immediate crisis support, call 988 and press 1.

For immediate emergencies: call 911. For veteran mental health crisis: call 988, press 1. Post 579 will respond to all requests as quickly as possible.

✅ Request Received — We Will Contact You

Your assistance request has been submitted to Post 579. A Service Officer or Post representative will reach out to you shortly. If this is an emergency, please call 988 (press 1) or 911.
