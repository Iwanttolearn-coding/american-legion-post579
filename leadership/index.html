Commander's Welcome Message

Commander's Message

American Legion Bicentennial Post 579 · Mrs. Carden Post

Welcome to Post 579 — one of San Antonio's proudest veterans' posts, serving the Lackland Terrace community since 1976. Our post carries the name of Mrs. Carden, a woman who embodied the spirit of service that defines The American Legion.

For nearly 50 years, Post 579 has been a home for veterans of all branches — a place where those who served can find fellowship, support, and community. Whether you're a longtime member or thinking about joining for the first time, you will find open doors and willing hands here.

Our mission is simple: to serve those who served. We advocate for veterans' benefits, support military families, mentor the next generation, and honor the memory of those who gave everything for this nation.

For God and Country.

— The Commander, American Legion Post 579

Elected Officers

Post 579's elected officers serve one-year terms and are responsible for the post's operations, programs, and community service efforts. Only approved officer information is published.

📸 Officer photos and updated bios are managed through the Officer Admin Dashboard. Submit your photo and bio to be published here.

👤
Commander

Post Commander

The Commander serves as the chief executive officer of Post 579, presiding over all meetings, representing the post at district and department functions, and overseeing all post operations.

👤
1st Vice Commander

1st Vice Commander

Assists the Commander and presides in the Commander's absence. Oversees membership recruitment and retention programs.

👤
2nd Vice Commander

2nd Vice Commander

Assists the Commander and 1st Vice Commander. Oversees post programs and community outreach initiatives.

👤
Adjutant

Post Adjutant

The Adjutant serves as the post's secretary and administrative officer — maintaining records, handling correspondence, and processing membership applications.

👤
Finance Officer

Finance Officer

Responsible for all financial records, dues collection, disbursements, and annual financial reporting in compliance with national American Legion requirements.

👤
Service Officer

Veterans Service Officer

Assists veterans and their families in filing VA claims, accessing benefits, and navigating the VA system. Free service to all veterans in the community.

👤
Chaplain

Post Chaplain

Provides spiritual support to members and their families, leads opening and closing prayers at post functions, and offers pastoral care during times of loss.

👤
Sergeant-at-Arms

Sergeant-at-Arms

Responsible for maintaining order during meetings and post events, assisting with setup and breakdown, and welcoming visitors at the door.
