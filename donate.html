Support Post 579

Your donation directly supports veterans, families, youth, and our community

Why Your Donation Matters

Every dollar donated to Post 579 goes directly back into serving veterans and the San Antonio community. We do not take a salary. We are veterans and volunteers serving veterans.

  • VA benefits counseling and claims assistance — free to all veterans
  • Veteran emergency assistance for housing, food, and utilities
  • Youth programs including American Legion Baseball and scholarships
  • Community service projects throughout San Antonio
  • Patriotic ceremonies honoring fallen heroes
  • Post facility maintenance and operations
  • Support for incarcerated veterans and reentry programs
  • Honor Guard services for veterans' funerals
