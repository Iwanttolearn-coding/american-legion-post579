Where Your Donation Goes

100% of donations to Post 579 stay in the local community, funding the programs and services that help San Antonio veterans and their families.

Veterans Assistance ProgramsFree VA claims help, benefits counseling, and crisis referrals for every veteran in our community.
👨‍👩‍👧
Youth & Scholarship ProgramsBoys State, Girls State, and scholarship support for deserving young men and women in our community.
🏛️
Post Facility MaintenanceKeeping the Post 579 hall and grounds clean, safe, and welcoming for veterans and community members.
🇺🇸
Patriotic Events & CeremoniesVeterans Day, Memorial Day, and other patriotic ceremonies that honor our nation's heroes.
