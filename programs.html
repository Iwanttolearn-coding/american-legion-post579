Programs

Youth, community, veterans outreach, and patriotism programs at Post 579

Post 579 Programs

The American Legion is committed to serving veterans, youth, and the community through meaningful programs. Post 579 supports and participates in the following.

American Legion Baseball

One of the most successful amateur athletic programs in the nation. American Legion Baseball has helped develop countless Major League Baseball players. Post 579 supports youth participation in this premier program.

Boys State / Girls State

A premier civic education program where high school students learn about state and local government through immersive, hands-on participation. Post 579 nominates and sponsors qualified local students annually.

Oratorical Contest

The American Legion National High School Oratorical Contest — a scholarship program and Constitution-focused competition that develops public speaking skills and patriotic values in young Americans.

Buddy Poppy Program

The Buddy Poppy is The American Legion's symbol of sacrifice. Our annual campaign honors fallen veterans and raises funds to support disabled veterans and their families in our community.

Community Service

Post 579 actively participates in community service projects throughout San Antonio — from food drives and cleanups to supporting local schools and families in need.

Veteran Outreach

We actively seek out veterans who may not know about their benefits or who need assistance connecting with VA services, community resources, and the American Legion network.

Honor Guard

Post 579 maintains an Honor Guard for military funerals and ceremonial events. We believe every veteran deserves a proper farewell with military honors.

Americanism Programs

Flag education, Constitution Day observances, essay contests, and civic programs that teach the values and history of the United States to students and the broader community.

Scholarships

The American Legion offers scholarship programs at the national and state level. Post 579 works to connect qualified veterans and students with scholarship opportunities.

Get Involved

Volunteer for Post 579 programs, sponsor a youth activity, or join as a member to participate in all we do.
