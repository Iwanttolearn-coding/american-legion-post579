Post 579 Programs

Youth, community outreach, patriotism, and veteran support programs at Bicentennial Post 579.

Programs We Support

Post 579 actively participates in national American Legion programs that serve veterans, youth, and our community in San Antonio.

Youth

Boys State

Premier civic education for high school juniors — Post 579 nominates and sponsors qualified students annually.

Youth

Legion Baseball

One of America's top amateur athletic programs. Post 579 supports youth participation in this historic program.

Scholarships

Oratorical Contest

National Constitution-focused scholarship competition developing public speaking and patriotic values.

Military

Honor Guard

Post 579 maintains an Honor Guard for military funerals and ceremonial events. Every veteran deserves honors.

Community

Community Service

Food drives, cleanups, school support, and community outreach throughout Lackland Terrace and San Antonio.

Memorial

Buddy Poppy

Annual campaign honoring fallen veterans and raising funds to support disabled veterans and their families.

Veterans

Veteran Outreach

Connecting veterans who may not know about their benefits with VA services and the Legion network.

Patriotism

Americanism Programs

Flag education, Constitution Day, essay contests, and civic programs teaching American values and history.

Scholarships

Scholarship Programs

Connecting veterans and students with national and state American Legion scholarship opportunities.

Get Involved with Post 579 Programs

Volunteer, sponsor a youth activity, or join as a member to participate in everything Post 579 does for veterans and the community.
