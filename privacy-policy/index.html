Privacy Policy

American Legion Post 579 — Mrs. Carden Post

Effective Date: June 2026

Information We Collect

American Legion Post 579 collects personal information only when voluntarily submitted through our website forms, including membership applications, contact forms, veteran assistance requests, and event registrations. This may include name, email address, phone number, military service information, and other information you provide.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted to Post 579 is used solely to process membership applications, respond to inquiries, coordinate veteran assistance, manage event registrations, and communicate Post 579 updates. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for commercial purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information you submit. All form submissions are transmitted securely and stored in a protected database. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting Post 579 directly at the contact information on our Contact page.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact Post 579 at commander@post579sa.org.
