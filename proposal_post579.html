Executive Summary

TM Designs™ proposes to design, develop, and deploy a complete professional website for American Legion Post 579 in San Antonio, Texas. This proposal covers the full scope of work required to establish a modern, mobile-responsive digital presence that serves Post members, veterans, the community, and prospective new members.

The website will serve as Post 579's official online home — enabling online membership applications, event announcements, hall rental inquiries, veteran assistance resources, donation processing, and officer communications from a single, professionally designed platform.
