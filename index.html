Our Mission

For God and Country — Post 579 stands for honor, service, and patriotism in every action we take.

🎖️

Honor Our Veterans

Every veteran who served receives recognition and a community that never forgets their sacrifice.

🤝

Community Service

From youth programs to patriotic ceremonies — Post 579 is woven into the Lackland Terrace community.

📜

Legislative Advocacy

We fight for veterans' rights, proper VA funding, and legislation that honors those who served.

🎓

Youth Programs

Boys State, Girls State, scholarships — investing in the next generation of American leaders.

🏥

VA Assistance

Free help with VA claims, disability, housing, employment, and mental health resources.

🎺

Patriotic Ceremonies

Memorial Day, Veterans Day, POW/MIA Recognition — we honor the fallen with dignity and pride.
