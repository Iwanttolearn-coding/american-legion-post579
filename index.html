Veterans
Serving
Veterans

Post 579's accredited Service Officers provide free, confidential VA claims assistance, benefits guidance, and resource referrals to every veteran in our community — regardless of membership status.

Request Veteran Assistance

VA Disability Claims

Free help filing, documenting, and appealing VA disability claims for compensation you've earned.

Benefits Counseling

Healthcare, education, home loans, pension — understand every benefit available to you.

Mental Health Support

PTSD resources, peer support, Vet Center referrals, and the Veterans Crisis Line (988, press 1).

Housing & Food Help

Emergency assistance referrals for veterans facing housing insecurity or food hardship.

Employment Services

Job placement referrals, vocational rehabilitation, and support for transitioning service members.

Incarcerated Veterans

Legal aid referrals, VA benefits restoration, and post-release reintegration support.

Join
Post 579

When you join American Legion Post 579, you join 2 million veterans in the most powerful veterans advocacy organization in history. You earn a voice, a community, and access to benefits you may not even know you have.

  • Free VA benefits counseling and claims assistance
  • Legislative advocacy at the federal and state level
  • Member discounts on insurance, travel, and more
  • Brotherhood and sisterhood of fellow veterans
  • Youth programs, scholarships, and community service
  • Participation in patriotic ceremonies and events
Join Now Renew Membership

Eligibility Requirements

You are eligible if you served at least one day of active federal military duty since December 7, 1941 with an honorable discharge — or are currently serving honorably.

  • United States Army
  • United States Navy
  • United States Marine Corps
  • United States Air Force
  • United States Coast Guard
  • United States Space Force
  • National Guard (federal activation)
  • Reserve components (federal orders)
