Our Mission at Post 579

Bicentennial Post 579 — Mr. Harold Post — has served veterans, military families, and the Lackland Terrace community since our charter in 1976. We stand for honor, service, and patriotism.

🎖️

Honor Our Veterans

We recognize and celebrate the service of every veteran from every branch who has defended our nation. No veteran stands alone at Post 579.

🤝

Serve Our Community

From youth programs to community service events, Post 579 is woven into the fabric of San Antonio's Lackland Terrace neighborhood.

🏥

Veteran Assistance

We connect veterans and their families with VA benefits, disability claims, housing resources, mental health support, and employment services.

🎓

Youth & Education

Through Boys State, Girls State, scholarships, and youth programs, we invest in the next generation of American leaders.

🏛️

Legislative Advocacy

We advocate at every level of government for veterans' rights, proper funding for the VA, and policies that protect those who served.

🎺

Ceremonies & Tradition

Memorial Day, Veterans Day, POW/MIA Recognition Day — we honor the fallen and celebrate American tradition with pride and dignity.

Honoring All Who Served

Post 579 proudly welcomes veterans from every branch of the United States Armed Forces. We are one family — one mission — one nation.
