About Post 579

Committed to Veterans and Community Service

Post 579 — Mrs. Carden Post — has served the Lackland Terrace community of San Antonio since its charter in 1976. For nearly 50 years, our post has been a home for veterans of all branches — a place where those who served can find fellowship, support, and purpose.

Our Post welcomes eligible veterans, active-duty service members, families, volunteers, and community supporters who believe in service, honor, and country. We operate as a 501(c)(19) veterans' organization, and every dollar raised goes directly to serving our community.

Membership

Join or Renew Your Membership

Membership helps Post 579 continue serving veterans, families, and the community. Members participate in meetings, events, service projects, advocacy, and fellowship.
