I. Purpose

This memorandum requests authorization for the purchase and installation of large-screen flat-panel television displays (96"–98" class) for the Post 579 main hall and secondary gathering areas at 3002 Gunsmoke Street, San Antonio, TX 78227. This upgrade will significantly improve the member experience during sporting events, memorial observances, post meetings, community fundraisers, and veteran outreach events.

Background: Post 579 currently hosts weekly gatherings, steak nights, fish fries, Veterans Day banquets, and community events. Member feedback has consistently identified upgraded display technology as the top facility improvement request. Modern large-format TVs will also increase ticket sales for events by enhancing the hall's commercial appeal.

IV. Financial Justification

Per 2020 Post 579 financial filings, the post reported approximately $241,000 in annual revenue, $146,000 in expenses, and $379,000 in assets. The proposed expenditure of approximately $4,060 represents less than 2% of annual revenue and less than 1.1% of total assets — well within acceptable capital improvement limits for a nonprofit of this size.

Recommended funding source: General Operating Fund or Facilities Improvement Reserve. No member dues increase required.

V. Authorization Request

The Facilities Committee respectfully requests the Commander's signature authorizing this purchase. Upon approval, procurement will be completed within 14 business days with installation scheduled within 30 days.
