Join Post 579

Become part of one of San Antonio's finest veterans' posts. Service. Fellowship. Community.

Who Can Join?

The American Legion is open to veterans who served honorably during eligible wartime periods. Associate membership is available for non-veterans who support our mission.

🎖️

Regular Member

U.S. veterans who served honorably on active duty during an eligible war era, or who served after December 7, 1941. Must have an honorable discharge.

⭐

Active Duty

Currently serving members of the U.S. Armed Forces on active duty status. Active duty members join free of National dues.

🤝

Associate Member

Non-veterans who believe in the American Legion's mission may join as associate members to support Post 579's programs and community service.

♾️

Life Member

Eligible veterans may purchase a lifetime membership — a one-time payment that covers dues for life and includes the Life Member lapel pin.
