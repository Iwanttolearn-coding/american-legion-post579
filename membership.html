Who Can Join?

Membership in The American Legion is open to any person who served at least one day of active federal military duty since December 7, 1941, with an honorable discharge — or is currently serving honorably on active duty.

  • United States Army
  • United States Navy
  • United States Marine Corps
  • United States Air Force
  • United States Coast Guard
  • United States Space Force
  • National Guard (under federal activation)
  • Reserve components (federal activation)

A copy of your DD-214 or equivalent discharge documentation will be required to verify eligibility.

Why Join Post 579?

When you join The American Legion, you join 2 million veterans in the most powerful veterans advocacy organization in history.

  • ★ Free VA benefits counseling and claims help
  • ★ Legislative advocacy on Capitol Hill
  • ★ Member discounts on insurance, travel, and more
  • ★ Community of fellow veterans who understand
  • ★ Youth programs, scholarships, and leadership
  • ★ Participation in patriotic ceremonies
  • ★ Local Post 579 events and activities

Dues information: Contact the Post Commander for current annual dues rates.
