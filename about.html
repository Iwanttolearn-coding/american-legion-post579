About Post 579

Serving veterans, military families, and the San Antonio community since 1976.

Our Story

American Legion Bicentennial Post 579 was chartered in 1976 in San Antonio's Lackland Terrace community. For nearly five decades, Post 579 has served as a home for veterans of every branch of the United States Armed Forces — a place of fellowship, service, and patriotic purpose.

Under the leadership of Commander Mr. Harold, Post 579 continues its mission to serve veterans, advocate for their rights, support youth programs, and strengthen the bonds of the community. We are proud to carry forward the American Legion's mission: "For God and Country."
